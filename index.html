Ultimate 4-Season Vacation Rental Homes


Snowbasin Vacation Rentals offers visitors 4 large luxury vacation homes located in the Trapper's Ridge development of Wolf Creek Resort.


TR122 (Big Horn—8 bedroom/ 5 bathrooms), TR50 (Wind River—8 bedroom/4 bathrooms), and TR63 (Wind River—5 bedroom/3bathrooms), TR123 (Big Horn–6 bedrooms/5.5 bathrooms) are well-maintained newer homes that are finely tuned for your 4-season vacation experience. Your large family or group can enjoy luxury and privacy in our amenity-rich homes. You will have access to Trapper’s Ridge swimming pool, hot tub, basketball, tennis and golf. You will enjoy the great outdoor activities that the Ogden Valley is famous for skiing, snowboarding, hiking, fly fishing… Then, come home to your well-supplied house with plenty of open space for all to relax!


Since 2006, Snowbasin Vacation Rentals has provided guests with well-outfitted homes at owner direct outstanding values.


Our goal is to care for each home as if it were our own and care for each guest as if they were our guests. We work very hard to offer an exceptional value to the guests while creating a greater return to the homeowner. If you are a homeowner interested in this level of service and return, we welcome new additions to our listings…please inquire.


Close to Utah's Best

Our rental homes are a short 55-minute drive from Salt Lake International Airport. Powder Mountain Ski Resort and its 10,000 acres of ultimate powder is just 10 minutes away. With no lift lines, you’ll have instant access to unlimited Utah powder. Snowbasin, a Sun Valley resort, is just 20 minutes away. With luxury lifts and lodge, Snowbasin was the home of the 2002 Olympic Alpine Downhill and Super-G Events. Snowbasin has terrain to suit all skiing abilities from beautiful long groomed runs as well as the best off-piste skiing you will find! An un-crowded Utah gem!

MORE POWD…LESS CROWD!

You’ll enjoy golfing at the Wolf Creek Championship Golf Course which is Audubon Society approved. www.wolfcreekresort.com/golf/ You will have access to The Club @ Wolf Creek. State-of-the-art fitness facility. $30/10 day pass: fitness.wolfcreekresort.com/ (not available at this time with Covid)

These homes are perfect for family reunions, multi-family fun, yoga retreats, business retreats, church retreats or girl’s or guy’s trips!!!

Hiking…fishing… Pine view Reservoir water sports… tennis…Trappers Ridge swimming pool……no end of recreation!
